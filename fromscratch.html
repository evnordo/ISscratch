The Championship 2020 Laker Team

A Story of Resilience During Uncertain Times



While the Lakers had been going through some years of rebuilding, entering the 2019-2020 season things had started to look up. With the acquisition of Anthony Davis, the team looked prepped to make a championship run. To the surprise of everyone, the Covid-19 virus shut down the world, and basketball came screeching to a halt while everyone tried to figure out how to handle it. The Bubble was created and basketball was back in business, leaving the question: "Who will take home this infamous bubble championship?"




The Road to the Championship

  1. vs. the Portland Trailblazers
    • The Blazers won Game 1
    • The Lakers won the next 4 games to eliminate them
  2. vs. the Houston Rockets
    • Once again the Lakers lost the first game
    • Came back to win 4 straight and advance to the conference finals
  3. vs. the Denver Nuggets
    • The toughest competition yet
    • The Lakers won the series 4-1
    • Anthony Davis hit a side out of bounds 3 to win Game 2
  4. vs. the Miami Heat
    • Took it to 6 games
    • Defeated the Heat and became the 2020 World Champs
